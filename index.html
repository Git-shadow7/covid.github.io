    PREVENTIONS OF COVID-19

    Stay Home

    Stay Home and Stay Safe. this is the best way to Safe from coronavirus.

    Wear Mask

    Wear a cloth face covering over your mouth and nose when around others.

    Wash Hands

    Wash your hands often with soap and water, especially after being in public places.

    Clean Surfaces

    Clean and disinfect Surface around your home and work frequently.

    Sneeze or Cough

    Cover your nose and mouth with a tissue or use your elbow and stay safe.

    Avoid close contact

    Keep distance at least 6 feet between yourself and others if you must be in public.
